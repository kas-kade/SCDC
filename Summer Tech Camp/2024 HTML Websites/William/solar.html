Solar system

Our solar system is made up of planet and a star in the middle which we call the sun.
There are 8 planets and 5 dwarf planets in our solar system.


List of planets in our solar system
(In order from closest to farthest from the sun.)

  2. Venus
  3. Earth
  4. Mars
  5. Jupiter
  6. Saturn
  7. Uranus
  8. Neptune
